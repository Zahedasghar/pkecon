Pakistan Industry — Two-Phase Activity
This activity has two distinct phases. In Phase 1, your group analyses evidence and builds a diagnosis. In Phase 2, you use that diagnosis to evaluate real policy proposals.
  • 1Phase 1 — Economic Autopsy (~12 min, unscored): You receive 6 evidence cards on Pakistan's industrial sector. Classify each, identify binding constraints, and write your group's diagnosis.
  • 2Phase 2 — Policy Stress Test (~10 min, scored): Four reform proposals are put to your group as a "tribunal." Based on your Phase 1 diagnosis, evaluate whether each will work — and explain why.
Phase 1 has no score. Its purpose is to make sure your group actually understands the evidence before being tested on it in Phase 2.
