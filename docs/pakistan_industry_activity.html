GROUP ACTIVITY — 2 OR 3 STUDENTS
Pakistan Industry Challenge
This activity has two phases. Phase 1 builds your diagnosis of Pakistan's industrial sector. Phase 2 stress-tests it with real policy proposals. Your results are submitted to your instructor automatically at the end.
  • 1Phase 1 — Economic Autopsy (~12 min, unscored): Classify 6 evidence cards, identify 2 binding constraints, write your group diagnosis.
  • 2Phase 2 — Policy Tribunal (~10 min, scored out of 8): Evaluate 4 reform proposals. Your score and diagnosis are submitted to your instructor automatically.
Phase 1 has no score. Its purpose is to ensure your group understands the evidence before being tested on it in Phase 2.
